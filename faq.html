Website Security & Management

How does Genkiz ensure secure website management?

  • We implement advanced security protocols, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and DDoS protection.
  • Our team performs regular software updates, security patches, and vulnerability scans.
  • We offer 24/7 monitoring and backup solutions to prevent data loss and ensure business continuity.
Website Performance & Maintenance

How does Genkiz keep websites running efficiently?

  • We optimize website speed through caching, CDN integration, and image compression.
  • Our team conducts regular performance audits and resolves technical issues before they impact users.
  • We ensure cross-device and browser compatibility for a seamless user experience.
Web Development & Training

Does Genkiz offer training or courses on website management?

  • Yes! We provide hands-on training sessions covering CMS management, SEO best practices, and security.
  • Our team offers personalized workshops to help businesses manage content updates and site performance.
Analytics & Data Insights

Can Genkiz provide insights on website performance?

  • We track key performance indicators (KPIs) like page speed, bounce rates, and conversion rates.
  • Our team provides detailed reports on user behavior and engagement to improve digital strategies.
  • We offer A/B testing and heatmaps to help businesses optimize website elements for better performance.
