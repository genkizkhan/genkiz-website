Our History

GENKIZ HISTORICAL GROWTH

2020
The Foundation
  • The team started with a single e-commerce project, helping a small business launch an online store.
  • The founder, an experienced web developer, handled everything from design, development, and SEO to ensure the store’s success.
  • To scale operations, the founder trained two junior developers.
  • The first year was dedicated to building expertise, streamlining workflows, and gaining client trust.
2022
Expansion
  • With a stronger team, Genkiz expanded its services beyond e-commerce to include website maintenance and custom web solutions.
  • The trained developers took on new projects, allowing the company to serve multiple clients while maintaining high-quality service.
  • The team experimented with agile workflows, automated backups, and performance optimization to improve website reliability.
2023
Specialization and Growth
  • Genkiz became recognized for its expertise in WordPress maintenance and custom web development.
  • The team grew further, taking on complex projects such as Gutenberg block-based plugins, API integrations, and UX/UI optimizations.
  • They continued mentoring new developers, refining internal processes, and expanding service offerings.
2024
Scaling with New Talent
  • To meet increasing demand, four new trainees were added to the team.
  • The company structured its operations by assigning mentors to guide new trainees and ensure quality output.
  • Genkiz expanded its service portfolio, including website security audits, speed optimization, and digital marketing support.
  • With a growing team, Genkiz started taking on larger projects, improving client retention, and strengthening its market presence.
